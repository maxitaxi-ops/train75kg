Daily Calorie Target

1,900–2,200

Roughly 500 kcal below maintenance. Maintenance for 85–90 kg + office work + training is ~2,400–2,700 kcal/day.

Don't go below 1,800 kcal — too deep a deficit kills muscle and energy for training.

Protein — Non-Negotiable

140–170g

1.6–2.0g per kg of bodyweight. Preserves muscle while losing fat. Chicken, salmon, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, lentils.

Hitting protein is more important than hitting a calorie number exactly.

Carb Timing

Eat most carbs around your training window. Oats before, rice or potatoes after.

Pre-workout (60–90 min before)Banana + protein
Post-workout (within 45 min)Rice + chicken
EveningsLower carb

Hydration

2.5–3.5L

Water per day. Add electrolytes for sessions over 45 min. Coffee is fine and can boost performance. Limit alcohol — it stalls fat loss significantly.

Weekend Strategy

Allow one social meal. Use Saturday's long session to offset. Keep alcohol to a minimum — even 2–3 drinks on Friday night can erase 2 days of deficit.

Consistency over 12 weeks beats perfection for 3 days then a blowout weekend.
